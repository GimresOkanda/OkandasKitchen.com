Classic Egg Sandwich Recipe

KITCHEN GEAR

  • Small bowl
  • Measuring spoons
  • Small nonstick skillet
  • Spatula
  • 2 Plates

INGREDIENTS

  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 tsp vegetable or olive oil
  • Place the cooked egg on the toasted English muffin to create a sandwich.
  • 2 whole-wheat English muffins, splitand toasted

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Crack one egg on the rim of the bowl and then empty it into the bowl. Throw away or compost the shell.
  2. Put the skillet on the stove and turn the heat to medium. Let it heat up for about 2 minutes, then add the oil.
  3. Slowly slide the egg from the bowl to one side of the skillet.
  4. Crack the second egg into the bowl, then slide it onto the other side of the skillet.
  5. When the eggs start to set and the edges are solid, use the spatula to flip the first egg, then the second egg.
  6. Continue to cook the eggs until the yolks are firm.
  7. Put an English muffin half on each plate. Slide an egg onto each muffin, then top with the other muffin halves. Serve right away.
