Frozen Mango Sherbet Recipe

KITCHEN GEAR

  • Food processor
  • Measuring cups
  • Wooden spoon

INGREDIENTS

  • 4 cups frozen mango chunks
  • 2/3 cup plain yogurt

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Put the mango chunks in the bowl of the food processor fitted with a steel blade and process until it looks like shredded cheese.
  2. Add the yogurt and process again. At first it will spin around and stay icy, but keep at it, stopping the machine and using the wooden spoon to loosen the mixture if it stops moving. At some point, it will suddenly go creamy and custardy, like soft-serve ice cream. Serve right away.
